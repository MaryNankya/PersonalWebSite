Hello!

I am an Adjunct Professor at Fitchburg State University, Computer Science Department. I hold a Masters Degree in Computer Science from Fitchburg State University, and bring a rich background in both academia and industry.

My research interests are in the broad areas of cybersecurity, AI, ML, wireless communication and networks, and emerging technologies such as 5G, 6G, and beyond networks. In addition to these areas, I am deeply passionate about the application of Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques in improving revolutionising industries like health care systems, industrial controm systems as well as strengthening their security from Cyber attacks .

I am currently working in the industry as a Data and Evaluation Manajor and publishing research in the above mentioned topics .
